Ricardo Chavarriaga (CLAIRE Office Switzerland) (Apr 02 2020 at 08:08):

#SciDipTalks | The COVID-19 Crisis in Europe: A Failure of Science Diplomacy?

This session aims to analyze the main differences between member states' responses to the pandemic and scrutinize the role of the EU in coordinating national efforts and advancing virus mitigation. It will finally reflect on the internal challenges the virus has brought to light, and the long-term impact on the European integration process.

Speakers

  • Alexis Roig. SciTech DiploHub (Barcelona Science and Technology Diplomacy Hub)
  • Pol Morillas. Director of the Barcelona Centre for International Affairs (CIDOB)
  • Sara Cebrián. Science and Innovation Attaché of the British Embassy in Madrid
  • Ivo Šlosarčík. Jean Monnet Chair in EU Politics and Administration at Charles University in Prague and Research fellow at S4D4C
  • Moderator: Carme Colomina. Research Fellow at Barcelona Centre for International Affairs (CIDOB) and journalist at Diari Ara
