PostDocs and PhD positions in trustworthy AI

The Algorithmic Decision Theory group at UNSW Sydney and Data61 is expanding. We have, in the first instance, four additional 5-year PostDocs and eight PhD positions. Positions start Jan 2021 or later. Competitive salaries and working conditions. Please contact Toby Walsh for details. tw@cse.unsw.edu.au
