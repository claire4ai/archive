Topic: Postdoc Opportunity at CiTIUS -Santiago de Compostela, Spain

{% raw %}

Alberto Bugarín (CiTIUS - University of Santiago de Compostela, ES) (Dec 12 2019 at 17:58):

CiTIUS-Research Centre in Intelligent Technologies of the University of Santiago de Compostela is looking for post-doc candidates with a good research record in its areas of interest. Particularly, applications from researchers in XAI, NLG and/or ML are welcome. We are looking for candidates with a good record of scientific publications in top conferences and scientific journals. We also expect self-motivated candidates who are able to work both independently as Junior Group Leader as well as a team player.

In this process, CiTIUS will select 2 postdoctoral candidates that will then submit their application to the new “Research talent attraction Programme” of the Galician Government, which offers the following conditions:

  • Full-time contract (4 years, 37,800EUR/year, gross salary)
  • Welcome and research support pack: total amount 160,000EUR (40,000EUR/year)
  • Possibility of Tenure-track position after completing the program (this depending on results assessment)

CiTIUS offers the possibility to conduct highly competitive research and active involvement in ongoing research projects. Also, Santiago de Compostela is a Monumental UNESCO World Heritage Awarded City, which offers a very high quality of life at a very affordable living cost for European standards.

Further info, application form and details at CiTIUS Website

DEADLINE: 26th December 2019 at 23h59 CET (UTC + 01:00)

Do not hesitate to contact me for any informal inquiry.

Best regards,
Alberto Bugarín
