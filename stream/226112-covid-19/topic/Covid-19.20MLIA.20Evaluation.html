Ricardo Chavarriaga (CLAIRE Office Switzerland) (Oct 22 2020 at 12:36):

Covid-19 MLIA Eval organizes a community evaluation effort aimed at accelerating the creation of resources and tools for improved MultiLingual Information Access (MLIA) in the current emergency situation with a reference to a general public use case:

We are ready to start the first evaluation round. We focus on multilingual information access for Covid-19 by offering three tasks: information extraction, multilingual semantic search, and machine translation.

Runs are due 27 November.

Details at: eval.covid19-mlia.eu
