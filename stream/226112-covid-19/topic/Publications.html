Ricardo Chavarriaga (CLAIRE Office Switzerland) (Apr 21 2020 at 14:59):

Special track (Faster Review and 1-year Free Access) in the IEEE Transactions on Artificial Intelligence (IEEE TAI), offering the AI community an opportunity to contribute to COVID-19.

The reviews of the manuscripts submitted to this special track will be expedited, to the extent possible, while ensuring that all papers will abide with the same rigorous review process for IEEE TAI.
If accepted, all such manuscripts will be published, free-of-charge to authors and readers, as free access for one year from the date of the publication to enable the research findings to be disseminated widely and freely to other researchers and the community at large.

Submit at https://mc.manuscriptcentral.com/tai-ieee

Please check authors instructions at https://cis.ieee.org/publications/ieee-transactions-on-artificial-intelligence

Please choose the COVID-19 Track during submission

IEEE Transactions on Artificial Intelligence - IEEE Computational Intelligence Society
From its institution as the Neural Networks Council in the early 1990s, the IEEE Computational Intelligence Society has rapidly grown into a robust community with a vision for addressing real-world issues with biologically-motivated computational paradigms. The Society offers leading research in nature-inspired problem solving, including neural networks, evolutionary algorithms, fuzzy systems, and hybrid intelligent systems. Members contribute to the theory, design, application, and development of biologically and linguistically motivated computational paradigms, emphasizing neural networks, connectionist systems, genetic algorithms, evolutionary programming, fuzzy systems, and hybrid intelligent systems in which these paradigms are contained.
