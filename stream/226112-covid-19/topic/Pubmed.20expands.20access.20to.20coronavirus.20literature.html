Topic: Pubmed expands access to coronavirus literature

Ricardo Chavarriaga (CLAIRE Office Switzerland) (Mar 26 2020 at 15:21):

The National Library of Medicine (NLM), part of the National Institutes of Health, is working on multiple fronts to aid in the COVID-19 response through new initiatives with the global publishing community and artificial intelligence researchers. NLM is expanding access to scientific papers on coronavirus for researchers, care providers, and the public, and for text-mining research.
