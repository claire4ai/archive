Topic: CLAIRE/AIHub - AI for Good series

Ricardo Chavarriaga (CLAIRE Office Switzerland) (Jan 04 2021 at 15:54):

Last month CLAIRE successfully started its collaboration with AIhub on the “AI for Good” focus series. The current content is focused on SDG 3: “good health and well-being”

The COVID-19 taskforce is contributing to this topic with several articles. Two of them are already published and there are more to come. You can find the published articles here: https://aihub.org/tag/focus-on-good-health-and-well-being/

Since the AIhub finds the SDG topic “good health and well-being” very crucial, they decided to prolong it for another month.

Are you interested in getting involved?

We are looking for researchers who work in healthcare and AI who are interested in writing or talking about their work. You could write (or describe on video) a short blog post about your research, write an opinion piece about an area of current interest, give a tutorial, or take part in an interview. If you are interested, please do get in touch at aihuborg@gmail.com.
