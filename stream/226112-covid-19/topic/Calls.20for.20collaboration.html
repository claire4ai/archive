The Blue Brain Project: technology and expertise available to fight COVID-19

The COVID-19 pandemic is an unprecedented global emergency. With cases and deaths doubling every four days or less, delays cost lives. In these conditions, EPFL’s Blue Brain Project is offering its expertise and supercomputing resources, free of charge, to hospitals, COVID-19 researchers and decision-makers who can use them. We are open to proposals from anywhere in the world. Read what we have to offer. If you need help, ask!
