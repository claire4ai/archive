As a service to the whole community, CLAIRE has set up a website to compile existing initiatives (projects, funding calls, hackathons, open datasets or tools, etc) by which AI and emerging technologies are contributing to combat COVID19. It also lists recent news about how CLAIRE members and the AI community as a whole is participating in these efforts.

Your help is needed to keep the website up to date. Through the website you also share new initiatives or volunteer in CLAIRE efforts
