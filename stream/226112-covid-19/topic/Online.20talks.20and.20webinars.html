Ricardo Chavarriaga (CLAIRE Office Switzerland) (Jun 29 2020 at 19:14):

IEEE Free Webinar "Does your COVID-19 tracing app follow you forever? Privacy & Protection in a post-pandemic world."

The COVID-19 crisis has created an unprecedented need for contact tracing across countries. Contact tracing is claimed to be an effective tool to break the chains of transmission of an infectious disease and is thus an essential public health tool for controlling outbreaks. One of the key concerns in these contact tracing apps has been the intrusion into data privacy and security including the exploitation by malicious actors and stalkers. This talk will compare different countries' approaches & critique the strength and weaknesses of these technologies.

9 July @ 3:30 PM IST | 8:00 PM Melbourne

Ricardo Chavarriaga (CLAIRE Office Switzerland) (Jul 09 2020 at 07:42):

In part celebration of our 2nd founding anniversary, we gladly present a webinar featuring results and way forward of the CLAIRE Task Force on AI and COVID-19 on 15 July 2020, 11:00 AM CET.

Following the launch of CLAIRE Task Force COVID-19 on 20 March 2020, more than 150 AI researchers throughout Europe collected and curated resources which aimed to leverage AI techniques in the context of COVID-19 and to support the development of new projects in several application areas. Under this task force, seven major groups were formed working on mobility and monitoring data analysis; bioinformatics; medical image analysis; social dynamics and networks monitoring; robotics; and scheduling & resource management.

The webinar will focus on discussing three-month task force outcomes in the areas of AI for bioinformatics, drug repurposing, and medical image analysis. It will feature experiences and insights of

  • Emanuela Girardi, Co-coordinator of CLAIRE and the COVID-19 Task Force;
  • Giovanni Stilo, Assistant Professor, Department of Information Engineering, Computer Science and Mathematics at the University of L'Aquila;
  • Andrea Beccari, Business Unit Head, R&D Platforms and Services of Dompé farmaceutici SpA;
  • Marco Aldinucci, Professor, Computer Science Department of the University of Torino;
  • Silvio Tossato, Deputy Head of Node ELIXIR-Italy.
