Topic: OECD Recommendation on Access to Data from Public Funding

Ricardo Chavarriaga (CLAIRE Office Switzerland) (Jan 22 2021 at 13:16):

In the context of the global COVID-19 pandemic, policies that enable the sharing of research data are important in helping accelerate the fight against the disease. This Recommendation provides guidance to governments on enhancing access to research data. For further information on this Recommendation and its relevance to COVID-19 response and recovery, see the background information below.

https://legalinstruments.oecd.org/en/instruments/OECD-LEGAL-0347
