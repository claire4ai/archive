Topic: Support from Finland!

Holger Hoos (Leiden University, NL) (Aug 30 2019 at 17:25):

Excellent news from Finland:
1) Finland has just become the 7th EU country whose government officially supports the #CLAIRE vision, and the first Nordic country to do so. Many thanks to our Finnish supporters who made that happen!
2) The Finnish AI Society, FAIS (official national representative of Finland in EurAI) has just pledged its support for CLAIRE.
3) Two national contact points for CLAIRE in Finland have been confirmed by FAIS: Matti Järvisalo (President of FAIS) and Petri Myllymäki (Vice-president of the Finnish Centre for AI).
A warm welcome to Matti and Petri in their new role as national contact points. It's great for all of us to have Finland so strongly involved!
