INRIA AND DFKI SIGN MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING FOR COOPERATION IN AI

On the first anniversary of the Aachen Treaty, the French National Institute for Information Technology, Inria, and the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence, DFKI, signed a Memorandum of Understanding - including a strong commitment to CLAIRE.
