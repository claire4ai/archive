Hi All,

The Global AI Ethics Consortium (GAIEC) and the Institute for Ethics in Artificial Intelligence are very excited to announce the launch of the GAIEC Repository. The GAIEC Repository has the goal of promoting research on AI ethics and making this information more easily available to all. It centralizes data, publications and other work from our members and those working on AI ethics, broadly.
