Mauro Vallati (University of Huddersfield, UK) (Mar 12 2021 at 19:33):

We are seeking to appoint a Research Fellow to work, together with Mauro and part of the PARK research group, on the UKRI funded project "Artificial Intelligence for Autonomic Urban Traffic and Control" ( https://www.ai4utmc.info/ ).
We are already collaborating with industry and local authorities to design and deploy the next generation of traffic control systems, empowered by cutting-edge AI. This is a very exciting topic, and can lead to game-changing solutions both on the application and on the theoretical sides. Having a positive impact on the real world is always a very satisficing experience :)
You won't be working on the software development side, but you will support the research side, and be expected to publish at major international venues. You'll have your expenses covered for travelling at conferences, when (if?) that will be allowed again.

Additional information about the position can be found here:
https://vacancies.hud.ac.uk/tlive_webrecruitment/wrd/run/ETREC107GF.open?VACANCY_ID=2934596yTG&WVID=3887655noN&LANG=USA

Given the circumstances, we can also discuss remote working solutions.

For additional information, or for arranging an informal chat, please drop a line to Mauro at m.vallati@hud.ac.uk

Cheers,
