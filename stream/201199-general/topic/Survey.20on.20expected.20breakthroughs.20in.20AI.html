Topic: Survey on expected breakthroughs in AI

Ricardo Chavarriaga (CLAIRE Office Switzerland) (May 13 2021 at 20:28):

The Geneva Science and Diplomacy Anticipator (GESDA) aims to leverage the International Geneva ecosystem to anticipate, accelerate and translate into concrete actions the use of emerging science-driven topics.

GESDA is conducting a survey to identify the most exciting and anticipated breakthroughs or advancements in AI that have not yet been realized and could be expected in the next 25 years. In particular in areas like transparent and trustworthy AI, AGI, common-sense AI and unconventional approaches to AI.

By way of thanks for completing this survey, we would like to offer you a complimentary invitation to join GESDA's high-level summit on 7-9 October 2021 and participate virtually in dialogue with your peers about anticipatory science and the future impact of scientific breakthroughs.
