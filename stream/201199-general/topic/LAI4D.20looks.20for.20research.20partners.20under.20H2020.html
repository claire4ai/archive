Topic: LAI4D looks for research partners under H2020

Juan Andrés Hurtado (Laboratory of Artificial Intelligence for Design, ES) (Apr 16 2020 at 11:15):

The Laboratory of Artificial Intelligence for Design is looking for partners to form a consortium under the EU research and innovation funding program Horizon 2020. The proposal ODAL (Organic Design Automation Lab) is an ambitious R&D project in the domain of AI applied to the design of physical systems. It is an opportunity to participate in a really innovative research challenge. If you think it could be of your interest you can find all details at http://odal.lai4d.org
