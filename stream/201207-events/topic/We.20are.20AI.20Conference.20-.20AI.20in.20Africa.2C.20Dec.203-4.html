Andreas Matt (IMAGINARY, DE) (Dec 02 2020 at 07:27):

The African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS) and Quantum Leap Africa, in partnership with IMAGINARY, invite you to the free Virtual Conference and Exhibition "WE ARE A.I. - Into the world of Artificial Intelligence.”

Organized on the side-lines of the virtual Next Einstein Forum (NEF) with the theme: “Building Africa’s resilience through education, research, and innovation”, this online event aims to spread awareness of Artificial Intelligence, its technologies, advances, and applications. It’s open to everyone interested in AI: Teachers, students, and anyone with curiosity will find interactive sessions with games and digital exhibits; those with more knowledge or experience can participate in panels and presentations about AI and its African perspective for education, research, and communication.

Register for free and join us on December 3 and 4, 2020 at http://we-are-ai.org.

We especially invite the African public and members of the African technology and research community to participate and showcase how Africa is preparing for the 4th Industrial Revolution through science and innovation.
