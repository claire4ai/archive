AI applications for evidence management - survey

Dear all,

Our colleagues at PwC EU Services EESV have launched a survey that aims at investigating AI applications for evidence management in order to support the European Food Safety Authority (EFSA) in developing a harmonised approach on the implementation of AI methods to the evidence management phase of the risk assessment. Answering the survey can be an opportunity to contribute shaping the future EFSA roadmap for implementation of AI in its processes and get in touch with the organisation.

You can access survey by clicking the following link: https://ec.europa.eu/eusurvey/runner/AI-in-evidence-management_2021#page0

The survey takes 10 minutes on average to complete and closes the 12th of September 2021.

Please feel free to forward it to those colleagues of yours that you think might be appropriate or interested.

In addition, they invite you to a webinar organised within the context of the “Roadmap for actions on artificial intelligence for evidence management in risk assessment” project that they are carrying out for EFSA. It will take place online on July 27 2021, from 11.00 to 12.00 CET. The webinar will be a chance to have an insight on EFSA vision on AI application and future opportunities.

Please click on the following link to register: https://www.efsa.europa.eu/en/events/webinar-use-ai-evidence-management-efsas-vision

For any questions, please do not hesitate to contact the PwC team (giovanna.galasso@pwc.com, luca.david@pwc.com, costanza.bersani@pwc.com).
