Dear all,

With a line-up of excellent speakers and talks, we invite you to the first event in our Leibniz AI Lab Workshop Series, https://leibniz-ai-lab.de/, on June 10-11 on the theme “Harnessing Big Data for Precision Medicine and Healthcare”.

Medical research, diagnostics and therapy are increasingly IT-supported and networked. Huge amounts of heterogeneous routine care data are already being collected in hospitals, e.g., in intensive care units, as
well as in primary care institutions and by citizens in their personal environments. In addition, the use of genomics, metabolomics and other omics data can provide insights into the development of illnesses and
suitability for specific treatments, which enables individualized therapies and treatment. Through the series of workshops in Leibniz AI Lab we aim to build a community involving researchers from machine learning, AI, biology, medical professionals and more to address the shared questions.

The event is online and open for participation to students and researchers at any stage of career including professionals in industry. The talks are organized under four interesting sessions on computational
methods in medicine, mental health, computational methods in medicine and epidemiology.

Please register at our webpage

https://leibniz-ai-lab.de/?etn=upcoming-events

to participate. Registration is free of charge. Schedule and details about the talks are available at the webpage.

We will have two sessions, both on June 10 and 11, from 9am - noon and 3pm - 6pm, German time.
