1st INTERNATIONAL FORUM on DIGITAL and DEMOCRACY. Towards a sustainable Evolution

December 10-11, 2020

We have the pleasure to invite you to the first International Forum on Digital and Democracy, December 10th - 11th,2020.
The Forum brings together politicians and academia to address the concern on how technology is challenging democracy.

The event is promoted by Associazione Copernicani, Re-Imagine Europe along with the endorsement of the European Commission, UNESCO, and SDSN.
The goal of the Forum is to gain a broader understanding and a shared view of the digital transformation impacts and how they are affecting liberal and democratic values, with a special focus on the pandemic impact.

The Forum will be opened by the keynote by Hon. Věra Jourová, European Vice-President for Values and Transparency.
She will unveil the European Democracy Action Plan, the European Commission flagship initiative that seeks to strengthen democracy and promote digital literacy.
Her intervention will set the scene for the two-day discussions engaging among the others sociologist and Spanish Minister of Universities Manuel Castells, former EU President Romano Prodi, and public policy analyst Jeffrey Sachs.

Peer-reviewed academic papers will address and discuss pressing questions about the impact of AI, blockchain, and ADM on political decisions, social involvement, the functioning of parties, information access, and integrity.

The forum will be fully online.

Further information and registration form available at https://www.ifdad.org
