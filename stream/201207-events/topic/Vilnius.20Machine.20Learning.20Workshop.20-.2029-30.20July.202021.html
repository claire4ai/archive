Topic: Vilnius Machine Learning Workshop - 29-30 July 2021

Alexa Kodde (CLAIRE Headquarters, NL) (Jul 02 2021 at 07:59):

Dear all,

On behalf of Linas Petkevičius, Board member of the Artificial Intelligence Association of Lithuania, we would like to invite you to join the Vilnius Machine Learning Workshop.

Vilnius Machine Learning Workshop (VMLW, 29-30 July 2021) is a two-day workshop aimed to popularise topics related to Deep Learning, Reinforcement Learning and Causal Inference among students and practitioners. It is organized by Artificial intelligence association of Lithuania as a satellite event to EEML summer school. The overall goal of these events is to support the regional Machine Learning community.

We invite not only doctoral students, researchers, summer workshops, but also all AIs who are sympathetic and want to get to know:

#generativemodels, #causalinference, #reinforcementlearning.
The two-day event will be remote & free, will include hands-on sessions and interesting presentations from new research from DeepMind, GoogleBrain, Mila - Quebec Artificial Intelligence Institute, University of Oxford.

Register: https://workshops.eeml.eu/
