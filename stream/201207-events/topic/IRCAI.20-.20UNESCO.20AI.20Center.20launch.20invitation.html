IRCAI - UNESCO AI Center launch invitation

Dear friends and colleagues,

We would like to invite you to join us on March 29 and 30 at the launch of the new UNESCO Category II Centre, the International Research Centre in Artificial Intelligence (IRCAI).

This is our new endeavor in creating a collaborating partnership with AI researchers and practitioners excited and committed to creating impact in the key challenges of our age.

It is our great pleasure to announce that Her Excellency, Ms Audrey Azoulay, Director-General of UNESCO and His Excellency Mr. Borut Pahor, President of the Republic of Slovenia will be present and open this event.

Please register here https://ircai.org/launch-2021/

To kick-it off we are also launching a call for the World’s Most Promising Artificial Intelligence Projects advancing the United Nations SDGs.

We invite you to apply with your research projects across companies, labs and universities alike, and showcase to the public how AI from all corners of the world can create value for humanity and impact in sustainability related challenges.

See the call and application form here https://ircai.org/global-top-100/

Join us as there's more in the programme, it'll be great meeting you next week!

John Shawe-Taylor,
Director at IRCAI
