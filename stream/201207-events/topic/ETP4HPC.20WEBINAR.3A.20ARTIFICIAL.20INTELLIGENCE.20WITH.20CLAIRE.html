Topic: ETP4HPC WEBINAR: ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE WITH CLAIRE

Joost Geurts (Dec 14 2020 at 10:37):

15 December 10 to 11am CET
https://www.etp4hpc.eu/events/etp4hpc-webinar-artificial-intelligence-with-_556.html

ETP4HPC organizes a joint webinair with the CLAIRE network (https://claire-ai.org/) to further explore AI use-cases, and possible collaborations in the context of the TransContinuum Initiative (TCI). TCI describes the defining characteristic of the infrastructure required for the convergence of data and compute capabilities in many leading edge industrial and scientific use scenarios (see https://www.etp4hpc.eu/transcontinuum-initiative.html). Prof. Dr.-Ing. Philipp Slusallek, co-founder and Member of the Board of CLAIRE, will present CLAIRE and show us the multiple facets of “AI”: the main domains and workflows where AI is being heavily used. Typical work- and dataflows in our daily lives and in the industry are influenced at many different stages by AI methods and algorithms. An abstracted view of these stages will help to position the value of AI in our digital continuum.
