4th AI-based Apps and Videos Competitions
19th Conference of the Spanish Association for Artificial Intelligence (CAEPIA)
September 22-24, 2021 in Malaga, Spain

The 19th Conference of the Spanish Association for Artificial Intelligence (CAEPIA) organizes the competitions on AI-based apps and AI informative videos, with the aim of highlighting the practical importance of artificial intelligence (AI), and its dissemination to all types of audiences.

In the app competition, fully functional applications (iOS, Android), developed either in Spanish or English, where AI is the key are admitted.

In the video competition, four categories have been established for:
• new informative videos
• new videos promoting STEM vocations
• new videos acknowledging the role of women in AI
• pre-existing videos that promote the use and/or development of AI techniques

Also videos in Spanish or English are accepted.

The explanatory documents of both apps and videos accepted for the final phase of both competitions will be published, for wider dissemination, in the CAEPIA proceedings along with the rest of the papers accepted in the other conference activities.

Important dates for these competitions are:

  • Submission of expression of interest: 10 May 2021
  • Submission of the final version of the app/video description document: 20 June 2021
  • App/Video submission: 7 September 2021
  • Final phase of the competition (at CAEPIA20-21): 22-24 September 2021

All detailed information, including rules, requirements and submission forms are available at:

https://caepia20-21.uma.es/competition_en.html

In addition to encouraging you to participate in both competitions, we thank you for all the diffusion you can make of this call.
