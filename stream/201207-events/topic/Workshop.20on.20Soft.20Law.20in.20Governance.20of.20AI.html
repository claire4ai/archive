Topic: Workshop on Soft Law in Governance of AI

Ricardo Chavarriaga (CLAIRE Office Switzerland) (Oct 01 2020 at 11:51):

The Center for Law, Science and Innovation at the Sandra Day O'Connor College of Law invites observers to register for the October 9th workshop on the role of soft law in the governance of artificial intelligence (AI).

Soft law is defined as any program that creates substantive expectations, but is not enforceable by government. For decades, it has shaped the governance of emerging technologies. The objective of this event is to examine diverse aspects in the nexus between soft law and AI. Panelists will present papers submitted to the Center and observers can participate in the discussion via the Q&A.
