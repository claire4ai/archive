Alexa Kodde (CLAIRE Headquarters, NL) (Jan 30 2020 at 09:17):

Free Entrepreneurship Workshop for Researchers (6th of February in Brussels)

Together with the National University of Ireland Galway, IESE Business School, Venture Hub, RomaTre University and Translated, the European Young Innovators Forum (EYIF) are implementing the Erasmus+ project, STARTED.

As part of the project they are organising a Free Entrepreneurship Workshop for Researchers to Empower Them to Create Innoven-Driven Enterprises. The training will take place in Brussels on the 6th of February (from 9.30am to 1.30pm).

Registration: https://www.eventbrite.co.uk/e/free-workshop-for-researchers-on-creating-innoven-driven-enterprises-tickets-90760965491

The workshop is researcher-focused and is part of EYIF's mission to reinforce and structure a European network for promoting entrepreneurship and innovation in R&D. With this training, delivered by members of the STARTED project, EYIF wants to empower researchers and equip them with a set of entrepreneurial skills, to aid their development. The workshop is a great opportunity to gain new, practical entrepreneurship skills and also to network with other innovators.
