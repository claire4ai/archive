Topic: AI4EU/StairwAI Workshop"How we can bring Value to your SME?"

Alexa Kodde (CLAIRE Headquarters, NL) (Jun 16 2021 at 14:47):

We are glad to spread the workshop organized by AI4EU and StairwAI "How we can bring Value to your SME?" that will be held online, on 25th of June, 12.00 CET.

SMEs are an important backbone of the European economy and AI is a promising technology that brings value to people, society, environment and business.

To combine both, Europe is funding dedicated projects that help to bring AI to SMEs by running concrete initiatives that support European SMEs in developing and adopting AI innovation.

In this context, the two projects AI4EU and StairwAI are organizing with selected SMEs a series of consultancy sessions to jointly learn how to better support SMEs in using AI technology to improve their business.

You are invited to join the kick-off workshop of 60 min that will give you a brief overview of AI and AI use cases and how to get access to needed AI expertise. It will be complemented with a short introduction of the DDI Framework that is used by companies and innovators to develop new AI-driven business cases.

In addition, you will be inform how to apply for a free AI Business Consultancy session for your company in which a team of AI experts will investigate together with your team how to use AI to improve your business and you will be first informed when the StairwAI €2M grant will be released through open calls later this year

Through the consultancy session your company will develop concrete new business ideas and get guided access to a wide range of AI assets offered by European research labs and universities. And the two mentioned projects will learn how to scale up their support to a wide range of European companies. So a clear win-win situation.

We are looking forward to welcome you at the 25th of June at 12:00 CET.
