How to solve the balance between the need for privacy and the use of tracking to monitor outbreak evolution is one of the many challenges brought by the current situation.
Current responses by different countries can be a litmus tests on existing attitudes in this respect. See for instance: http://www.top10vpn.com/news/surveillance/covid-19-digital-rights-tracker/
