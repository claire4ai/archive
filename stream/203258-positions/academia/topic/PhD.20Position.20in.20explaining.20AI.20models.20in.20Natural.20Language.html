Topic: PhD Position in explaining AI models in Natural Language

Alberto Bugarín (CiTIUS - University of Santiago de Compostela, ES) (Jul 01 2021 at 15:48):

Dear colleagues,

The following PhD position is offered within the framework of the “NL4XAI: Interactive Natural Language Technology for Explainable Artificial Intelligence” MSCA-ITN project:

  • PhD research topic: From grey-box models to explainable models
  • Main objective: To define, design, develop and implement an agnostic-based approach for providing Natural Language explanations of Bayesian Networks
  • Main supervisor Prof. Dr. Alberto Bugarín-Diz, Centro Singular de Investigación en Tecnoloxías Intelixentes (CiTIUS) – Universidade de Santiago de Compostela (USC)
  • Co-Supervisor: Prof. Dr. Ehud Reiter, Department of Computing Science– University of Aberdeen (UNIABDN)

We look for outstanding, motivated and team-spirited candidates to carry out a PhD within the NL4XAI ITN and who will get unique international and inter-sectoral training from prominent European researchers (from both academy and industry). The PhD will be based at CiTIUS-Univ. Santiago de Compostela, with planned secondments at Univ. Aberdeen (Ehud Reiter) and at Minsait company (Hitoshi Yano). Young researchers willing to work on Explainable AI are encouraged to apply.

Closing date for applications is 29 August 2021. Further details at: https://nl4xai.eu/open_position/esr2_2021/. Applications must be made through the NL4XAI website, but feel free to contact me if you have any questions.

Also, feel free to forward this call to anyone who might be interested.
