Topic: Fellowship Opportunities: AI4EO ‘Beyond Fellows’

Anastasia Aritzi (Institute for Ethics in AI, DE) (Feb 15 2021 at 09:38):

Dear all,

Through its “Beyond Fellow” program, the Ethics team of the AI4EO lab now invites Ph.D. candidates and early Postdocs from diverse disciplines to visit the Future Lab for a period of 3 to 6 months. The goal is to collaborate with the highly multi-disciplinary and international team to identify and research ethical issues and opportunities emerging from the utilization of Artificial Intelligence for Earth Observation.

Application Deadline: February 28th, 2021

-Download the call and learn more: https://www.linkedin.com/feed/update/urn:li:activity:6766984861820436480/
-More information about the AI4EO: https://ai4eo.de/

Please share this post with anyone who might be interested.

Best wishes,

Anastasia Aritzi
