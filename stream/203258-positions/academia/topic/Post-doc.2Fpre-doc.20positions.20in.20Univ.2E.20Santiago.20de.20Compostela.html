The University of Santiago de Compostela is looking for researchers on Computational Linguistics, Natural Language Processing, Artificial Intelligence, and related fields to join the Nós Project, aimed at building Artificial Intelligence resources and tools for Galician.
This call includes 3 postdoctoral (research associate) and 2 junior (research assistant) positions, for backgrounds in both NLP and Linguistics, all of them for a 3-year term.
