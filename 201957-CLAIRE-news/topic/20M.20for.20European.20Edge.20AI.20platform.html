Topic: 20M for European Edge AI platform

{% raw %}

Fred Buining (Nov 29 2019 at 11:25):

HIRO-MicroDatacenters, Dutch SME with a focus on Edge computing for Big Data analytics and AI as a service, can report a strategic win.

20M Investment for development of European Edge AI platform. An Industry lead, European Consortium commits to developing the platform and demonstrate it in Smart Hospitals, Cities and Factories around Europe. The purpose-built mobile HIRO-MicroDataCenters will have AI functions running on application, system and embedded levels of the distributed federated HIRO-MicroDataCenter mesh. The European Edge AI platform will deliver unprecedented Performance/ Watt, Edge Scalability, Security but also Privacy and Data Sovereignty.
The project received financial support from the EU(5M) and 14 EU members states(5M).
More information will be released coming months.

HIRO-MicroDataCenters will also be attending the European AI summit in Brussels next week. http://knowledge4innovation.eu/
