Way to go, Scotland! "Scotland is getting a state-of-the-art research center which could help transform manufacturing"

National Manufacturing Institute Scotland will be operated by the University of Strathclyde and is backed by the Scottish government and organizations including Scottish Enterprise, Renfrewshire Council and the Scottish Funding Council. Strathclyde is investing £8 million in the facility, while the Scottish government is putting in £48 million.
