Emanuela Girardi (Sep 01 2020 at 14:48):

“The Best Practice Forum on Data and New Technologies in an Internet Context”, a program of the United Nations Internet Governance Forum, launches a call for case studies related to the collection and usage of user data.

The best practices will feed into the BPF's discussions and workshops as part of the IGF 2020 intersessional work and the virtual IGF2020 meeting in November to inform and inspire the policy-makers.

The Best Practice Forum (BPF)
As co-facilitator of the Best Practice Forum 2020, I am asking you to help us collect case studies about Data and New Technologies. Please submit your projects related to this subject and promote the initiative within your organisations.

Here is the link to submit your case study: https://www.intgovforum.org/multilingual/content/bpf-data-new-technologies-case-studies
The deadline is 25 September 2020.

Thanks,
Emanuela
