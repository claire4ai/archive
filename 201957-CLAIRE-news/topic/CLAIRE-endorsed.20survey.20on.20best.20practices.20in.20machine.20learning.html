Topic: CLAIRE-endorsed survey on best practices in machine learning

Holger Hoos (Leiden University, NL) (Feb 14 2020 at 14:07):

CLAIRE officially endorses this new survey on best practices in machine learning:

se-ml.github.io/survey/

We encourage all our members and supporters who work in or with machine learning to participate, and to help us spread the word, especially to colleagues and former students in industry and public administration. Results will be shared here. Similar, high-quality efforts for different areas of AI are always welcome!
