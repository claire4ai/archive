Stream: CLAIRE news

Topic: NL AI research agenda and strategic action plan

{% raw %}

Holger Hoos (Leiden University, NL) (Dec 07 2019 at 17:09):

The Netherlands now have a national AI research agenda and a strategic action plan. The former can be found at https://www.nwo.nl/en/news-and-events/news/2019/11/first-national-research-agenda-for-artificial-intelligence.html and involves major contributions from CLAIRE supporters in NL; in particular, the 12-person editorial committee included CLAIRE key suppporters Inald Lagendijk (chair), Antal van den Bosch, Koen Hindriks, Holger Hoos, Maarten de Rijke, Bart Verheij and Aimee van Wynsberghe. The latter can be found at https://www.rijksoverheid.nl/documenten/beleidsnotas/2019/10/08/strategisch-actieplan-voor-artificiele-intelligentie and prominently mentions CLAIRE (pp. 26, 29 and 56).

Holger Hoos (Leiden University, NL) (Dec 07 2019 at 17:10):

Please let us know of any other national AI agendas or strategic action plans that mention CLAIRE and/or are based on contributions from CLAIRE supporters or member groups/institutions.
