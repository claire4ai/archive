Holger Hoos (Leiden University, NL) (Aug 31 2019 at 14:50):

Researchers from 20 CLAIRE member groups and institutions, working closely with several ELLIS supporters and other outstanding scientists, just won one of the largest and most prestigious grants in Dutch science. The 10-year, 19M EUR Gravitation project is on hybrid intelligence - AI that aims to augment, rather than replace, human intelligence. This is great news for AI in NL, for AI in Europe, and for CLAIRE.
