Anna Tahovská (CLAIRE Office, CZ) (Jan 23 2020 at 10:19):

Hi everyone,

I would like to start activities on this channel with an introduction of the CLAIRE Prague Office.

The office was officially opened on the 26 September 2019, during the RICAIP Kick off ceremony (ricaip.eu/2019/09/27/ricaip-kickoff-ceremony/), but has been in operation since early 2019.
The office is located at the Czech Technical University in Prague in CIIRC building. Currently there are 3 personnel working at this office - the head of the office, Vít Dočkal and two other members - Markéta Iffland and myself.

We would like to make this stream a place for sharing local information concerning AI, ideas and other relevant information.

Please feel free to do so and in case of any needed information and/or support, please, do not hesitate to contact anyone from the CLAIRE Prague office.

Best regards

Anna Tahovská
