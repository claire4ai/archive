Mitra Baratchi (Sep 01 2020 at 20:02):

Dear colleagues,

Please note that the submission deadline of BNAIC/BENELEARN 2020 (https://bnaic.liacs.leidenuniv.nl/) is now extended to 8 September 2020.

On behalf of the organizing committee that will be held in Leiden on 19-20 of November 2020, we invite you to consider submitting your research papers. The situation around COVID-19 has impacted society in many ways. As BAIC/BENELEARN 2020 organizers, we are carefully evaluating the situation, following the government line, and aim to provide a conference that is convenient for everyone to participate. According to the government plans, during the allocated dates meetings of up to 100 persons will be allowed, with the possibility of further relaxations. If this regulation does not change, we aim to host BNAIC/BENELEARN 2020 in a hybrid form combining on-line and on-site participation. Note that due to the specific COVID-regulations in November, the scale of the physical event could be smaller than usual.

Regards,

Jan van Rijn and Mitra Baratchi on behalf of the organizing committee

IMPORTANT INFORMATION

Paper submission deadline: 8 September 2020 (used to be August 28, 2020)
Author notification: October 5, 2020
Camera-ready submission deadline: October 26, 2020
All deadlines are at 23:59, AoE time zone.
Conference: November 19-20, 2020

TOPICS OF INTEREST
The conference accepts publications in the broad areas of AI and Machine learning. A list of topics is available in the following website: https://bnaic.liacs.leidenuniv.nl/

BNAIC/BeneLearn 2020, Leiden
bnaic.liacs.leidenuniv.nl
The reference AI & ML conference for Belgium, Netherlands & Luxemburg

PRIZES
Just like previous years, there will be prizes for the best type A paper (regular paper), the best demonstration, the best type D paper (thesis abstract) and the best poster.

PRE-PROCEEDINGS & POST-PROCEEDINGS
All contributions should be written in English, using the Springer CCIS/LNCS format (see http://www.springer.com/gp/computer-science/lncs/conference-proceedings-guidelines ) and submitted electronically via EasyChair: https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=bnaicbenelearn2020
Submission implies the willingness of at least one author to register for BNAIC/BENELEARN 2020 and present the paper.
Further information on pre and post-proceedings (Springer’s CCIS) are available on our website https://bnaic.liacs.leidenuniv.nl/
