**How can we shape AI research and developments for the benefit of individuals and society?
This is a central question for us in the European Project HumanE AI Net.**

Please join us on Wednesday, Dec 16th, 2020 from 17:00-19:00 CET Online (4pm-6pm GMT / 11am-1pm EST / 8am-10am PST) for a moderated online panel were we will discuss the vision and plans of the Humane AI Net project.

The project coordination team (Paul Lukowicz, Virginia Dignum, and John Shawe-Taylor) and experts in law and human centered-computing (Mireille Hildebrandt and Albrecht Schmidt) will share their view of how AI in Europe can be advanced while maximizing the value for individuals and society.

The meeting will take place online using Zoom. More details and the link to the event is available at: https://www.humane-ai.eu/event/vision2020/
