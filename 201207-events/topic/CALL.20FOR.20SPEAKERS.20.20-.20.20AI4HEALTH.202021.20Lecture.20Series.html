Alexa Kodde (CLAIRE Headquarters, NL) (Jan 21 2021 at 18:01):

CALL FOR SPEAKERS

AI4HEALTH 2021
Lecture Series
.

  • What's it all about?
    Promising innovations in Artificial Intelligence on the one hand and in the field of medicine on the other have led to increased interest in the frontier area AI/Medicine in recent years. As in many other fields, the application of Machine Learning and the digitalisation is at the forefront, but topics such as Ethics, Healthcare and a system's reliability have also found their way into the debate on the topic of "AI for humans". In addition, many colleagues have successfully shown in the context of various applications that AI and Medicine can be brought together in a meaningful way. The aim of the lecture series, therefore, is to give practitioners, researchers, engineers and scientists a platform to present their work.
    .

  • Organisation
    The time of the presentation can be freely chosen by each speaker. However, as last year, we plan to hold the presentations on Wednesday afternoon at 16.00 Luxembourg time. Due to Corona, it is planned that the lectures will take place virtually. There will be 50 minutes for the lecture plus 10 minutes for the discussion afterwards.

In case of interest, you may please contact me with your name and affiliation, the suggested title, a brief abstract, and the suggested date. More information: https://acc.uni.lu/ai4health/

Best regards,
Christoph Schommer, Dept of Computer Science
