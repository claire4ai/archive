WIPO Conversation on Intellectual Property (IP) and Artificial Intelligence (AI): Second Session
The second session of the WIPO Conversation on IP and AI will be held over three days from July 7 to 9, 2020 as a virtual meeting, in three daily sessions from 13:00 to 15:00 CEST, to allow the broadest possible global audience to attend. This virtual meeting is open to all interested parties. Further information regarding the structure of the meeting is available in the Q&A.
