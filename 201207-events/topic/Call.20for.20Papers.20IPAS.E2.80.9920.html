Extended Deadline
Fourth IEEE International Image Processing, Applications and Systems Conference
http://ipas.ieee.tn/
December 16-18 2020, Genova, Italy

The international Image Processing Applications and Systems conference is intended for grouping from all over the world challenging researchers, innovators, academicians, and practitioners in image processing theory and tools, for following high level tutorials, sharing their achievements, exchanging their experiences and discussing future orientations. The conference will also offer an opportunity to make a bridge between image processing researchers and people working in other application fields such as medical doctors, radiotherapists or industrial parts.
Accepted papers will be submitted for publication in IEEE Explore.

IMPORTANT DATES
Paper Submissions June 30, 2020
Special Session Proposal June 10, 2020
Special Session Acceptance July 15, 2020
Paper Acceptance September 15, 2020
Camera ready paper submission September 30, 2020
Author registration November 10 2020

REGULAR PAPER SUBMISSION

The submission and reviews for IPAS 2020 will be handled through the Microsoft Conference Management System. CMT – IPAS2020.
Authors can submit Full papers. Accepted papers will be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore.
https://cmt3.research.microsoft.com/IPAS2020/Submission/Manage

Manuscript length: 6 pages maximum for Full Paper Submission. This is a strict limit for initial submission. During final submission of accepted papers, authors may purchase extra pages if they find it necessary to address the concerns of the reviewers.
Double blind reviewing: IPAS follows a double-blind review process. Authors are required to include their names and affiliations in a separate document and omit their names and affiliations in their papers as illustrated in IPAS Conference templates. These templates (Microsoft Word and LaTeX) can be found at the following link:

IEEE Manuscript Templates for Conference Proceedings
Ethical Requirements for Authors:
The conference committee requires that all authors wishing to submit a paper declare that:
• The paper is substantially original and that no paper substantially similar in content has been submitted or will be submitted to any other conference or journal during the IPAS 2020 review period.
• The paper does not contain any plagiarism.
