Alexa Kodde (CLAIRE Headquarters, NL) (Sep 02 2020 at 10:46):

Dear @all members, supporters and friends of CLAIRE,

This Friday, 4 September, 10:45-12:15 CEST, CLAIRE will be running an on-line event at ECAI 2020 on the Future of European Excellence in AI. This will include updates on CLAIRE’s work towards fostering excellence in all areas of AI, across all of Europe, as well as an outline of next steps in achieving critical mass and global impact for "AI made in Europe”; there will also be ample opportunity to discuss what’s happening, as well as what needs to happen, at the European, regional and national level to reach those ambitious goals.

The event is open to everyone and does not require registration for ECAI 2020. It will give you an opportunity to ask questions and participate in discussions regarding CLAIRE's approach towards creating a bright and impactful future of European Excellence in AI.

To participate, please join us on Friday, 1 September, 10:45-12:15 at https://uio.zoom.us/j/68823677212 (Webinar ID: 688 2367 7212)!

Further details will be posted on the CLAIRE website (https://claire-ai.org/updates/) and on Zulip, but we believe that this will be of interest to you and members of your group or institution - so mark you calendar and feel free to share this invitation broadly.

See you on Friday,
Holger, Morten and Philipp (for the extended CLAIRE Core Team)
