Alexa Kodde (CLAIRE Headquarters, NL) (Nov 13 2020 at 09:51):

Open market consultation: Brussels Metro Pre-commercial procurement for smart monitor of people flow

MIVB-STIB invites interested bidders to participate in the open market consultation for its Muntstroom pre-commercial procurement. The aim is to develop novel data driven solutions for smart predictive monitoring of people flow, for use cases including transport, COVID-19 and security analytics. A Living Lab is envisaged for testing the solutions in the Brussels Capital Region. The solutions are needed for the Collection, Combination, Storage, Analysis of and Smart Access to people flow-data.

Register to the 19/11/20 webinar before 13/11/20 and complete the OMC questionnaire by 4/12/20.
