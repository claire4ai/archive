Francesco Masulli (Oct 05 2019 at 08:19):

ICAIH 2019 – First Industrial Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Health
How Artificial Intelligence is shaping the Future of Health
13-14 Nov 2019 – Palazzo Pirelli, Milan (Italy)
http://www.icaih.com/

SCOPE:
Artificial Intelligence is increasingly becoming an enabling technology for all areas of health and well being, ranging from bioinformatics, to drug discovery, clinics, and health care.
This conference aims to constituting a forum focused on the theme: “How Artificial Intelligence is shaping the Future of Health”, involving companies operating in the sector, start-ups, clinicians, researchers, and public administrators.
The event will be driven by questions like:
• What are today's most relevant applications of Artificial Intelligence in the health sector?
• What are the most reliable and suitable Artificial Intelligence technologies now available for organizations operating in the health sector?
• What are the medium-term development prospects of the health sector thanks to the applications of Artificial Intelligence?
ICAIH 2019 proposes a format including keynote presentations, round tables, sponsored session, poster sessions, and speed pitches.

PARTICIPATION:
Companies will participate in the quality of event sponsor (see Sponsor Program at http://www.icaih.com/sponsor/)
Free participation is granted to start-ups in the early stage founded after 2016, university and research center researchers, professionals, journalists, and public administrators. The registration form is available online at http://www.icaih.com/registration/ which is mandatory for all participants.

ROUND TABLES:
ICAIH will include the following round tables:
Round Table 1: AI in Bioinformatics and Drug Discovery
Round Table 2: AI in Clinics
Round Table 3: AI in Health Care
Round Table 4: AI in Well Being
Round Table 5: Education in AI and Health
Round Table 6: EU and Italian Initiatives in AI and Health
At the round tables, experts from the sponsoring companies, researchers and start-uppers will present the the AI technologies for the health sector available today, and their medium-term development prospects.
Researchers of universities and research institutes and start-upper interested in speaking to a round table must send an abstract of 500 characters (including spaces) of their contribution following the format of file round-table-contribution.doc (downloadable at https://tinyurl.com/y45ce7bg) by 15 Oct 2019 through the website of the submissions https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=icaih2019

SPEED PITCHES SESSION
On Nov 14 there will be a Speed Pitches session where researchers and start-uppers will present their activities and results in 3 minutes (with the support of a maximum of 5 slides). Researchers of universities and research institutes and start-upper interested in presenting a speed pitch must send an abstract of 500 characters (including spaces) of their contribution following the format of file speed-pitch.doc (downloadable at https://tinyurl.com/yxu9pljt) by 15 Oct 2019 through the website of the submissions https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=icaih2019

PUBLICATION:
Class Editori is preparing a magazine dedicated to ICAIH 2019 with articles dedicated to sponsors, presentations of round tables and cards presenting research groups and startups.
Research groups and start-ups interested participating at ICAIH 2019 must send their card in English (and if possible also in Italian) following the format of files card-research-group.doc (downloadable at https://tinyurl.com/y6kh84nn) or card-start-up.doc (downloadable at https://tinyurl.com/y6cdnue3) by October 31 2019 through the website of the submissions https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=icaih2019

IMPORTANT DATES:
15 Oct 2019: proposals for round table speeches & speed pitches
25 Oct 2019: acceptation of proposals for round table speeches & speed pitches
31 Oct 2019: submission of cards for the ICAIH 2019 magazine
13-14 Nov 2019: Conference
