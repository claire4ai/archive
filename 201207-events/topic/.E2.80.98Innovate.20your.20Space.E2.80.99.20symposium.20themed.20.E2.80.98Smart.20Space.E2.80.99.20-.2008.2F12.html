Alexa Kodde (CLAIRE Headquarters, NL) (Nov 16 2020 at 16:12):

After a successful symposium last year, we are pleased to invite you for our upcoming online TUD-TNO-SRON ‘Innovate your Space’ symposium themed ‘Smart Space’.

We want to focus in this symposium on needs, technologies and approaches for Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning in the Space Sector and beyond.

We are happy to announce as keynote speakers Raffaella Franco (ESA/ESTEC) and Prof. Dr.Thomas Bäck (Leiden Institute of Advanced Computer Science), who will share their insights on the symposium topic, representing two highly relevant organisations.

During a panel discussion we aim to trigger an exchange of ideas on how the various members of the Dutch space and non-space ecosystem can jointly collaborate on this exciting theme.

At the pitch rounds TUD, TNO and SRON researchers will be given the opportunity to pitch their ongoing research on the latest technological innovations.

The program can be found on the website of the TU Delft Space Institute. Please stay up to date since minor changes might occur.

The symposium will take place online from 09:00-12:15 on Tuesday December 8th 2020.
