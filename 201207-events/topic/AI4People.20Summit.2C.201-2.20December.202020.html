AI4People Summit, 1-2 December 2020

The AI4People Summit, now in its fourth edition, will present the 7 AI Global Frameworks. The 2020 edition follows the Towards a Good AI Society Summit opened by Tony Blair in November 2018.

The Summit will draw on the results of the AI4People’s 2020 activity and will engage in discussion leading representatives from policy makers, business and academia.

In 2020, starting from the “7 Key Requirements for a Trustworthy AI” published by the European Commission in April 2019, AI4People’s Committees members have assessed the impact of these requirements on 7 business sectors (Automotive, Banking and Finance, Energy, Healthcare, Insurance, Legal Services Industry and Media and Technology) whilst having suggested some practical steps that businesses operating within these industries must undertake to be compliant with the Requirements.
