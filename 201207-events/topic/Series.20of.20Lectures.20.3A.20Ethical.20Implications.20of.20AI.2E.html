Z-Inspection: A Holistic and Analytic Process to Assess Ethical AI
(Prof. Roberto V. Zicari)

Time: May 27, 2020 at 02:00 PM Amsterdam, Berlin, Rome, Stockholm, Vienna

Join Zoom Meeting
https://us02web.zoom.us/j/81785418938?pwd=VjlIbzJ1bG9abC9tTStLY2xhWGlDUT09

Meeting ID: 817 8541 8938
Password: 234362

Note: we will start the lecture at 2pm (CET) sharp.

In this lecture Prof. Roberto V. Zicari will present a novel holistic and analytic processes to assess Ethical AI, called Z-Inspection . Z-Inspection is a general inspection process for Ethical AI which can be applied to a variety of domains such as business, healthcare, public sector, etc. It uses applied ethics. To the best of our knowledge, Z-Inspection is the first process that combines a holistic and analytic approach to assess Ethical AI in practice.

The goal for creating Z-Inspection was to help contribute to closing the gap between “ principles” (the “what” of AI ethics) and “ practices” (the “how”), and hopefully help to foster ethical values and ethical actions.
