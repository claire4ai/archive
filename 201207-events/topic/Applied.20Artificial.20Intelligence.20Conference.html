Alexa Kodde (CLAIRE Headquarters, NL) (Apr 07 2020 at 14:39):

VIRTUAL CONFERENCE: Artificial Intelligence in Business - International B2B Conference on 11 May 2020

The Applied Artificial Intelligence Conference is an Austrian B2B event connecting AI users and AI solution providers around the world. At this year’s conference users will exchange their experiences gained from real AI use cases in business.

The Applied Artificial Intelligence Conference takes place on 11 May 2020 for the 3rd time already. About 500 participants from over 25 countries are expected. This year the conference will be held virtually.

Among the participants are renowned international experts such as Dr Eng Lim Goh, Senior Vice President of HPE, and computer pioneer from the very beginning, and from Montreal, one of the top AI hotspots worldwide, Alex Shee will attend as keynote speaker - COO of Element AI, one of the fastest growing companies in this field.

What else can you expect?
• Use cases of Artificial Intelligence for businesses
• Prearranged virtual 1:1 meetings - see who’s taking part and create your own schedule!
• Online panel discussions on the topics export success, sustainability & ethics and location & competition
• Virtual workshops
• +500 participants from all around the world

Pre-arranged virtual B2B meetings between participants and solution providers, users and experts from all business sectors will be organized by the Enterprise Europe Network Austria via the new B2match tool for virtual meetings.

More and more companies are considering the use of intelligent self-learning machines that can help them to drive their sales and profits. Using the right technologies in the right place efficiently can give companies the decisive competitive advantage. Find out what these developments can mean for your company at the Applied Artificial Intelligence Conference!

Participation is free of charge. However, you need to register here: https://aaic2020.b2match.io/signup and publish your online profile.
Please make sure to select the correct time zone upon registration.
