Anna Tahovská (CLAIRE Office, CZ) (Sep 11 2020 at 11:45):

At the European Council summit in July, the EU’s national leaders agreed on a compromise about the EU long-term budget that entails a massive cut to the planned budget for the next framework programme Horizon Europe, and potentially for the ERC.
A potentially disastrous threat for the future of Europe’s research, at a time when research is needed more than ever and when political leaders claim that the success of their policies will depend on research and innovation. There is a considerable gap with the ambitions expressed.

See the ERC President Jean_Pierre Bourguignon speech on this topic: https://erc.europa.eu/news/hearing-itre-european-parliament

The research community and the ERC are currently working very hard to achieve an important revision of this compromise. You can help by signing the petition "Protect the European Research Council (ERC) in the EU budget".

:memo: Sign the petition here: http://chng.it/LjxcdZKXgf

Read more about the topic: https://www.bbc.co.uk/news/amp/science-environment-54019253

:speaking_head: Please, do not hesitate to talk about this pressing issue with your MEPs and politicians in your country.:speaking_head:
