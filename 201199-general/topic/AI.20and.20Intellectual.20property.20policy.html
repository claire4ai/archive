In December 2019, WIPO opened a public consultation on the topic of AI and Intellectual Property policy. It received more than 250 submissions that can be read here: http://www.wipo.int/about-ip/en/artificial_intelligence/news/2020/news_0003.html

Further discussions in the topic will be held in Geneva in May 2020

About IP
Intellectual property (IP) refers to creations of the mind, such as inventions; literary and artistic works; designs; and symbols, names and images used in commerce
