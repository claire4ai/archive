Topic: Call for SMEs to join Focus group on AI

{% raw %}

Ricardo Chavarriaga (CLAIRE Office Switzerland) (Oct 14 2020 at 09:52):

The European DIGITAL SME Alliance has launched a call for applications for SMEs across Europe, including Switzerland, to join their Focus Group on AI. The focus group will help the Joint Research Centre monitor the impact of EU initiatives. The companies in the focus group will be able to provide feedback on the uptake of AI in industry and shape European AI policies. They are thus looking for SMEs with high expertise in AI willing to provide inputs and meet 1-2 times a year with the group over a period of 3-5 years. Of course this is also an opportunity to network with other AI innovators.

The deadline for application is this Friday, 16th of October!

https://www.digitalsme.eu/european-focus-group-on-artificial-intelligence/

SME Focus Group on Artificial Intelligence - European Digital SME Alliance
