Ricardo Chavarriaga (CLAIRE Office Switzerland) (Feb 08 2021 at 16:47):

The project NGI Explorers, funded by the EC, has launched its 3rd Open Call.
Next Generation Internet (NGI) Explorers is an exciting fellowship program, supported by the European Commission, with a €1 Million fund for Technology Expeditions to the US.

FOR WHO?
The Call is open for high profile Internet Researchers or Innovators (hi-tech startups, SMEs) from all over Europe and H2020 associated countries that are specialised/working on:

:black_medium_square: Artificial Intelligence
:black_medium_square: Blockchain
:black_medium_square: Big Data
:black_medium_square: Internet of Things
:black_medium_square: Cybersecurity
:black_medium_square: Cloud/Edge Computing
:black_medium_square: 5G

WHAT WE OFFER?
· 100% sponsored 3-5 months Technology Expedition in US
· Work directly with a US partner to accelerate your idea
· Free mentorship support
· Access to Europe’s most powerful network of Internet leaders - Next Generation Internet
· The chance to solve an Internet technology challenge(s) defined by our partners in the US!

APPLY NOW! Researchers and Innovators can select 1 of 3 tracks available to join the NGI Explorers program.

Deadline: 18 February 16:00 CET

Learn more: NGI Explorers website
