Ricardo Chavarriaga (CLAIRE Office Switzerland) (Jun 23 2020 at 08:03):

AI for Sustainable Development Goals (AI4SDGs) Think Tank (https://lnkd.in/ggXWR55). The AI4SDGs Think Tank is an online open service for everyone, a global repository and an analytic engine of AI projects and proposals that impacts #unsdgs, both positively and negatively. The goal is to promote the positive use of AI for Sustainable Development, and to investigate on the negative impact of AI on Sustainable Development. You are welcome to share your project to the world and get evaluated by the crowds.
Associated to the AI4SDGs Think Tank, the AI4SDGs Research Program is released and will open to everyone for applications (https://lnkd.in/g-vWJKS).
