Ricardo Chavarriaga (CLAIRE Office Switzerland) (Feb 27 2020 at 09:11):

The Organisation for Economic Co-operation and Development (OECD) will launch today the OECD Policy Observatory on Artificial Intelligence, an online platform to shape and share AI policies.

You can find more info and a link to a webcast of the event in this link: http://www.oecd.org/going-digital/ai/
