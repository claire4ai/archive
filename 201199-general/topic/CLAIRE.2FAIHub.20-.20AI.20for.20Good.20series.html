Anna Tahovská (CLAIRE Office, CZ) (Nov 24 2020 at 09:22):

CLAIRE and AIhub partner to launch focus series on “AI for Good”
We are excited to announce that, in partnership with CLAIRE, AIhub.org (https://aihub.org/) will be launching a focus series on “AI for Good” which will specifically concentrate on AI research relating to the UN sustainable development goals (SDGs). Each month we will focus on a different one of the goals and highlight work in that area by researchers in the field.
To start the series, we will focus on the SDG “good health and well-being”.

Are you interested in getting involved?
We are looking for researchers who work in healthcare and AI who are interested in writing or talking about their work. You could write (or describe on video) a short blog post about your research, write an opinion piece about an area of current interest, give a tutorial, or take part in an interview. If you are interested, please do get in touch at aihuborg@gmail.com.

About AIhub.org
AIhub.org is a non-profit dedicated to connecting the AI community to the public by providing free, high-quality information. We are supported by a number of leading AI organisations: CLAIRE, AAAI, NeurIPS, ICML, AIJ/IJCAI, ACM SIGAI, and RoboCup.
