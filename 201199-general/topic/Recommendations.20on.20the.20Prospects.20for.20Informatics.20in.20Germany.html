CLAIRE Office Germany (Oct 29 2020 at 06:59):

Dear all,

In its autumn meetings from 21 to 23 October 2020, the German Council of Science and Humanities discussed and adopted the"Recommendations on the Prospects for Informatics in Germany". They are now available for download at the following address: https://www.wissenschaftsrat. en/download/2020/8675-20.pdf.

The Science Council has analysed the development and current situation of academic informatics and identified three challenges which it has made recommendations for addressing. Personnel shortages are central to all areas. However, the topic is also the handling of the high dynamics in development inside and outside the discipline as well as the fact that computer systems and products now permeate all areas of life.

Printed copies of the recommendations can be requested by e-mail (beer@wissenschaftsrat.de) from the office of the German Council of Science and Humanities.
