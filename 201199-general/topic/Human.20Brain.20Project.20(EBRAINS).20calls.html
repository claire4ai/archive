The Human Brain Project (HBP) launches four Calls for Expression of Interest (CEoIs) for new projects to directly contribute to the development of the research infrastructure (EBRAINS) and increase the scope of its application in terms of innovation, neuroscience and clinical research. Click here for more information

The following Calls for Expression of Interest (CEoI) will be opened soon:

  • Brain Atlas and simulation engine adapter construction
  • Application of functional architectures supporting advanced cognitive functions to address AI problems of industrial relevance
  • High-level neuro-symbolic processing for guidance of goal-directed behaviour
  • Industry Engagement

For more information please contact info@opencalls.humanbrainproject.eu.

Ricardo Chavarriaga (CLAIRE Office Switzerland) (Aug 24 2020 at 19:01):

The deadlines for the Human Brain Project Calls for Expression of Interests are now available: https://www.humanbrainproject.eu/en/follow-hbp/news/calls-for-expression-of-interest-for-sga3-1/

Brain Atlas and simulation engine adapter construction

Budget: EUR 450,000 Direct Costs (plus 25% Indirect Costs). One proposal will be selected.
The pre-proposal submission deadline is 16 September 2020 17:00 Brussels time. The proposal submission deadline is 16 October 2020 17:00 Brussels time.

Application of functional architectures supporting advanced cognitive functions to address AI problems of industrial relevance
Budget: EUR 1,000,000 Direct Costs. Maximum funding per proposal: EUR 500,000 (plus 25% Indirect Costs). Two proposals will be selected.
The pre-proposal submission deadline is 16 September 2020 17:00 Brussels time. The proposal submission deadline is 16 October 2020 17:00 Brussels time.

High-level neuro-symbolic processing for guidance of goal-directed behaviour
Budget: EUR 450,000 Direct Costs (plus 25% Indirect Costs). One proposal will be selected.
The pre-proposal submission deadline is 16 September 2020 17:00 Brussels time. The proposal submission deadline is 16 October 2020 17:00 Brussels time.

Engagement of Industry, SMEs and start-ups
Budget: EUR 1,000,000 Direct Costs. Maximum funding per proposal: EUR 250,000 (plus 25% Indirect Costs). Four proposals will be selected.
The pre-proposal submission deadline is 16 September 2020 17:00 Brussels time. The proposal submission deadline is 16 October 2020 17:00 Brussels time.

Alexa Kodde (CLAIRE Headquarters, NL) (Aug 31 2020 at 09:31):

HBP SGA3 CEoI - Functional Architectures
Submission deadline 2020-10-16 15:00:00 (UTC)
https://opencalls.humanbrainproject.eu/call/hbp-sga3-ceoi-functional-architectures

The HBP Calls for Expression of Interest (CEoIs) are for new projects to directly contribute to the development of the research infrastructure (EBRAINS) and increase the scope of its application in terms of innovation, neuroscience and clinical research.

This CEoI is for organisations interested in the application of functional architectures to address AI and automation problems of industrial and commercial relevance, with special emphasis on vision.

Budget: EUR 1,000,000 Direct Costs. Maximum funding per proposal: EUR 500,000 (plus 25% Indirect Costs). Two proposals will be selected.

For more information, please read the Call documents.

The pre-proposal submission deadline is 16 September 2020 at 17:00 (Brussels time).
The proposal submission deadline is 16 October 2020 at 17:00 (Brussels time).
