Topic: UNESCO recommendation on Ethics for AI - Public consultation

{% raw %}

Ricardo Chavarriaga (CLAIRE Office Switzerland) (Jun 29 2020 at 19:12):

UNESCO started the consultation process for the world first normative instrument on Ethics of AI. The document is open for public consultation till July 31st, 2020

This instrument was elaborated by 24 independent experts appointed by the UNESCO Director-General and presents the result of several discussions on values, principles and policy actions for the design, development and deployment of AI. The instrument emphasises:

  • the concerns of low-income countries,
  • the welfare of present and future generations,
  • the impact of AI on the environment,
  • the 2030 Sustainable Development Agenda,
  • gender and other biases,
  • inequalities between and within countries, and leaving no one behind.

The deadline for submitting any feedback is July, 31st, 2020. This instrument is available in six languages and can be downloaded from https://unesdoc.unesco.org/ark:/48223/pf0000373434

To participate of the consultation process, in english, please visit
http://survey.unesco.org/2020/index.php?r=survey/index&sid=661611&lang=en

or, in french, visit
http://survey.unesco.org/2020/index.php?r=survey/index&sid=661611&lang=fr

More information about this normative instrument and whole process can be found at https://en.unesco.org/artificial-intelligence/ethics
